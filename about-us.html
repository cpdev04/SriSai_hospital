Welcome to Sri Sai Hospital

Established in 2006 by founders Dr. Shyamala Thyagaraj and D M Thyagaraj, Sri Sai Hospital has been a beacon of hope and healing in the community for over a decade. Situated in Yelahanka, our hospital was founded with a vision to provide accessible, affordable, and high-quality healthcare services to individuals and families in need. Over the years, we have grown from a modest clinic to a full-fledged healthcare facility equipped with state-of-the-art medical technology and staffed by a dedicated team of healthcare professionals.

Since our inception, Sri Sai Hospital has remained committed to its founding principles of compassionate care, integrity, and excellence. With a focus on patient-centricity and continuous innovation, we have expanded our services to meet the evolving healthcare needs of our community. From pioneering medical advancements to delivering personalized care, our hospital has earned a reputation for excellence and reliability. As we look towards the future, we remain dedicated to our mission of advancing health and enhancing lives, ensuring that every individual who walks through our doors receives the highest standard of care and support.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Sri Sai Hospital's FAQs cover common queries about services, appointments, insurance, billing, facilities, and more to ensure patients receive comprehensive information for their healthcare needs.

    Cancer Center FAQ

    • How do I schedule an appointment at Sri Sai Hospital?
      • You can schedule an appointment by calling our helpline or filling out the appointment form on our website. Our staff will assist you in finding a suitable time slot.
    • What are the visiting hours for patients?
      • Visiting hours are from 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM and 4:00 PM to 7:00 PM. However, exceptions can be made in special cases, and visitors are advised to check with the nursing station.
    • Does Sri Sai Hospital accept insurance?
      • Yes, we accept a wide range of insurance plans. Please bring your insurance card and any necessary documentation during your visit, and our billing department will assist you with the process.
    • Do you offer emergency services?
      • Yes, Sri Sai Hospital provides 24/7 emergency services. Our Emergency Department is equipped to handle all medical emergencies, and our expert team ensures prompt and efficient care.
    • Can I access my medical records online?
      • Yes, patients can access their medical records online through our patient portal. Simply register on our website, and you can view your medical history, test results, and upcoming appointments.
