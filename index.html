OBSTETRICS

Providing comprehensive maternal care, our clinic specializes in both normal and caesarean deliveries, conducting pregnancy scans and fetal anomaly scans to ensure the well-being of both mother and child. Additionally, we offer sterilization surgery and ethically managed MTP services.

Our value-added services include chromosomal mapping, karyotyping, genetic testing, and continuous fetal monitoring, all aimed at delivering the highest standards of care and peace of mind to expecting families.

GYNAECOLOGY Departments

Genworks equipment offers advanced colposcopy services, utilizing cloud storage for seamless data management. Additionally, our facility provides a range of surgical interventions including D & C, hysterectomy, cervical cancer surgery, ovarian cancer surgery, and breast cancer surgery to address various menstrual disorders and oncological concerns.

Our commitment to value-added services ensures patients receive comprehensive care, integrating cutting-edge technology with expert medical attention for optimal outcomes.

Read More

GENERAL & LAPROSCOPIC SURGERY

Surgeries for appendicitis, cholelithiasis, hernia repair (including umbilical hernia), piles (hemorrhoids), fistula, fissure, circumcision, and varicose veins are commonly performed using both open and laparoscopic techniques.

Additionally, procedures like FNAC and core needle biopsy aid in diagnosing conditions such as breast malignancy. These surgical interventions cater to a wide range of medical needs, ensuring comprehensive care for patients with various ailments.

INFERTILITY

Our specialized medical evaluation and treatment for infertility cater to both male and female patients, focusing on less invasive methods and affordable yet highly effective medical management.

With a strong emphasis on value-added services such as IUI, IVF, and ICSI, we ensure personalized care and support throughout the journey to parenthood, achieving a remarkable success rate. Trust us to navigate the complexities of infertility with expertise and compassion.
