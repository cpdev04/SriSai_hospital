Special Services

Sri Sai Hospital provides exceptional special services, offering advanced medical care, compassionate treatment, and personalized attention, ensuring optimal health outcomes for all patients with specialized needs.

General Medicine

Our adept physicians offer specialized care for diabetes, hypertension, and general health, ensuring comprehensive treatment for optimal well-being.

General & Laparoscopic Surgery

Our adept surgeons excel in open and laparoscopic procedures, ensuring patient safety and optimal outcomes for conditions like appendicitis and hernia repair.

OBG (Gynaecology)

Our obstetrics and gynecology department delivers top-tier care, ensuring women's health across all life stages.

 

Pediatric/Neonatology

Our dedicated pediatric team specializes in neonatology and pediatric medicine, ensuring comprehensive care for children from newborns to adolescents.

Orthopaedic

Our orthopedic specialists provide personalized care for fractures, sports injuries, arthritis, and joint disorders, enhancing mobility and life quality.

ENT (Ear, Nose, Throat)

Our skilled ENT specialists deliver comprehensive care for ear, nose, and throat issues, addressing sinusitis, hearing loss, and tonsillitis with precision.

Dermatology/Skin

Our dedicated ENT specialists deliver comprehensive care, addressing sinusitis, hearing loss, tonsillitis, and more with precision and expertise.

Plastic & Cosmetic Surgery

Our team of plastic and cosmetic surgeons is dedicated to achieving natural-looking results through safe, patient-centered procedures.

Cardiology

Our dedicated cardiology team delivers holistic cardiac care, expertly managing hypertension, coronary disease, and heart failure for optimal outcomes.

Medical & Surgical Oncology

Our oncology department delivers personalized cancer care with advanced treatments and compassionate support from diagnosis through survivorship.

Urology & Andrology

Our dedicated urology specialists provide tailored care for urinary tract and male reproductive issues, ensuring improved wellness for all.

Endocrinology

Specializing in hormonal disorders like diabetes and thyroid issues, our endocrinologists offer comprehensive care for optimal health.

Rheumatology

Our rheumatology department prioritizes early intervention, crafting personalized treatment plans to alleviate symptoms and enhance joint health effectively.

Pulmonology

Our pulmonology department provides specialized care for respiratory conditions, offering tailored treatments to enhance lung function and respiratory health.

Neurology

Our dedicated neurology specialists offer comprehensive care for conditions like stroke, epilepsy, and multiple sclerosis, ensuring optimal neurological health.

Bariatric Surgery

Our skilled bariatric surgeons provide tailored weight loss surgeries like gastric bypass and sleeve gastrectomy for lasting health improvements.

Intensive Care Unit (ICU)

Our ICU delivers unparalleled critical care, utilizing cutting-edge equipment and a collaborative team approach to safeguard patient well-being.

Physiotherapy

Our physiotherapy department delivers personalized rehabilitation services, enhancing mobility, strength, and well-being through specialized techniques and treatment plans.

Diagnostic Services

Utilizing cutting-edge technology and skilled professionals, our hospital delivers precise diagnostic services for informed patient treatment and care.

With pharmacy and lab services, a dialysis center, and a dental clinic on-site, patients receive comprehensive care in hygienic wards, ensuring their health needs are met efficiently and effectively.
